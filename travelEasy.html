TravelEasy Case Study

Any text here?

During my UX/UI course I had the opportunity to design a travel mobile app by the name of TravelEasy. The objective was to wireframe and create a prototype for an app that helps busy adults have a seamless travel experience. Based on user research conducted, I found out a lot of adults had a difficult time organizing and planning for a trip. I sought to address these issues by providing features not currently found on the market.

User Research

The goal was to understand user pain points when it comes to traveling and to provide solutions to those pain points.

Definition and Ideation

My user persona was Robert, a busy finance professional with limited time to go on vacatiions. He needs help staying organized while he is traveling.

Testing and Iterating

Conducted a competitor analysis on direct and indirect competitors to determine their strengths and weaknesses. Then created wireframes and prototypes based on user interviews and what was missing in the current market

Final Prototype

Gathered feedback from usability testing on digital protoypes and found users liked the aesthetic design of the app and found it easy to use. There was some confusion from the users about certain features; this was addressed by giving more details about them as well as adding more visuals.
